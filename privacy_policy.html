Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13/06/2025

ScreenOn Timer (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

The app does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, to enforce usage limits and ensure fair access, the app may:

  • Generate a non-personal, device-specific identifier (e.g., a hashed fingerprint of your device configuration).
  • Store this identifier on a secure remote server (e.g., Firebase) along with basic usage data (e.g., session counts, timestamp).

This data:

  • Cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Is used solely to monitor usage limits and unlock premium features if purchased.

2. Data Usage

The collected usage data is used only to:

  • Track free usage limits and prevent abuse (e.g., reinstalling the app to reset usage count).
  • Restore access to paid features if you reinstall the app after purchasing.
  • Maintain app stability and ensure fair access for all users.

We do not use this data for analytics, advertising, or any form of tracking outside of the app’s functionality.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to anyone.

We may store anonymized usage records using third-party services like Firebase, which are subject to their own privacy and security policies.

4. Internet Permissions

The app may access the internet to:

  • Sync usage data to prevent abuse of the free tier.
  • Restore premium purchases through Google Play Billing.

It does not access or transmit personal files, photos, contacts, or any other private data on your device.

5. Purchase Information

If you choose to make a one-time purchase to unlock unlimited usage, the transaction is processed securely via Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details.

6. Your Control

You may uninstall the app at any time. However, uninstalling or clearing app data may not reset usage limits if a device fingerprint has already been stored remotely to prevent repeated abuse.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected on this page and take effect upon publication.
